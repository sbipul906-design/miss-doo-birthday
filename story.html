Our Beginning ❤️

Sach bolu Miss Doo ❤️…

Jab maine tumhe pehli baar dekha tha…
Fresher induction ke time…
Main busy tha anchoring me… responsibilities me…

Par phir… maine tumhe dekha…

Aur sab kuch ek second ke liye ruk gaya…

Sabse pehle jo cheez notice ki…
wo thi tumhari smile 😊

Pata nahi kyu…
par us smile pe dil aa gaya ❤️


Aur phir dheere dheere…
tum bas ek ladki nahi rahi…

Tum ban gayi meri favorite person 💖

Tumhari aankhon me ek alag hi shine hai ✨
Jaise har baat bina bole samajh leti ho…

Tumhari smile…
itni pure hai ki pura din acha ho jata hai 😊

Tumhari awaaz…
itni sweet hai ki bas sunte rehne ka mann karta hai 🎧

Aur tumhara dil…
sach me bahut kind aur pure hai ❤️


Kabhi kabhi tum ek choti si bacchi lagti ho 🥺
innocent si… cute si…

Aur kabhi…
itni naughty ho jati ho 😏
ki bas tumhe dekhte rehne ka mann karta hai ❤️

Tumhari ye dono sides…
mujhe aur bhi zyada pasand hai 💖


"Na jaane kaise likhu tumhe…
tum koi lafz nahi… ek ehsaas ho…

Tumhari aankhein kahani likhti hain…
tumhari smile me sukoon sa ho…
